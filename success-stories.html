Oil Safety Tracking Software

Industry: Oil and Gas

Technologies: Python, TensorFlow, Flask, MongoDB, AWS, React

Our client, a major player in the oil and gas industry, aimed to improve safety standards and operational efficiency in their facilities. Leveraging the ScoreHMR (Safety Content Retrieval with Human Motion Recognition) model, we developed a cutting-edge safety tracking solution. The platform utilized video feeds from surveillance cameras to monitor and analyze safety protocols, worker movements, and hazardous situations in real time. The ScoreHMR model, powered by TensorFlow, processed video streams to identify potential safety hazards, such as unauthorized access to restricted areas, unsafe work practices, and equipment malfunctions. The platform alerted safety personnel to take immediate action, ensuring a swift response to mitigate risks. The safety tracking solution was instrumental in reducing workplace accidents and improving emergency response times. The platform's advanced analytics capabilities provided valuable insights for safety audits and process improvements. As a result, our client achieved a 40% reduction in safety incidents and a 30% increase in operational efficiency, setting a new benchmark for safety standards in the oil and gas industry.
